About Our Mosque

Al-Noor Mosque was established in 1995 to serve the growing Muslim community in our area. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for worship, education, and community building.

We offer daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah services, Quran classes for all ages, and various community programs throughout the year.
